THE ALCHEMY OF TIME

We reject the sterile uniformity of mass manufacturing. The Fusori Foundry operates at the intersection of ancient metallurgy and modern engineering.

Batch 001 introduces 'The Serpent'. Each 42mm case is not stamped, but meticulously sculpted and cast in-house using raw Sterling Silver 925 or Bronze 90/10. The resulting textures, imperfections, and natural patinas ensure that no two Fusori watches will ever be identical.

Beneath the sapphire crystal, a complex geometric dial is carved over hours on a desktop CNC using rigid V-bits, achieving a brilliant faceted texture without the use of fragile micro-tooling.

CASE MATERIAL

Cast Sterling Silver 925 or Bronze 90/10

CALIBER

Soprod M100 Automatic (H3 High Hand Height)

DIMENSIONS

42.0mm Diameter | 12.5mm Thickness

DIAL

34.0mm V-Carved Geometric Facets
